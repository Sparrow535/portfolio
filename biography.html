Biography

My name is Biran Rai, and I was born on March 22, 2000, in Bhutan. From a young age, I developed a deep passion for exploring new cultures and sharing my experiences with others. This passion has not only shaped my personal journey but has also guided my professional endeavors as a travel blogger, author, and speaker.

Growing up in Bhutan, I was surrounded by diverse cultures and breathtaking landscapes. This environment ignited my curiosity about the world beyond my hometown. I pursued an education that would equip me with the skills to explore and document my adventures. My academic background laid the foundation for a career that blends creativity with exploration.
